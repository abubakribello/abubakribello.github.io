The Problem

A construction-focused recruitment agency with 1,700+ client companies and 500 active job openings was struggling with scale. Recruiters were spending over 25 hours per week manually matching candidates to roles from a database of 20,000+ profiles.

The manual process was:

  • Slow and prone to human error
  • Inconsistent across different team members
  • Preventing business development activities
  • Creating a massive bottleneck for growth targets

The Solution

I designed an AI-powered matching engine that evaluates every candidate against open roles using sophisticated structured criteria.

Technical Implementation

  • Matching Engine: Built with Python and OpenAI to analyze CVs and job descriptions in seconds.
  • Multi-Dimensional Scoring: Candidates are scored across six dimensions including skills, experience level, location, and salary expectations.
  • Automated Logging: The system provides transparent match explanations for recruiter final review.
  • Seamless Integration: Connected directly to the existing candidate database and job board tools.
