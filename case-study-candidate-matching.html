AI Candidate Matching System

Cutting shortlisting time from 2 days to 16 hours across a database of 20,000+ profiles.

Client ToPeople
Database size 20,000+ profiles
CRM RecruitCRM
Impact 90% time saved

The situation.

ToPeople's recruiters were manually searching through a database of 20,000+ candidates every time a new vacancy came in. For each role, they'd spend hours filtering, reading profiles, and shortlisting by hand. Shortlisting typically took 2 days from job posting to a list of qualified candidates being ready.

With multiple new roles arriving each week, this wasn't sustainable. Recruiters were spending most of their time in the database rather than with clients or candidates. Quality also depended heavily on individual recruiter knowledge of which criteria actually mattered.

What I built.

I built an automated matching engine that evaluates every candidate-job combination against a consistent set of criteria and surfaces the best matches without any manual searching.

  • Dual triggers: The workflow runs on two events. When a new or updated candidate enters RecruitCRM, they're evaluated against all open jobs. When a new or updated job is added, all candidates are evaluated against it.
  • 5-criteria evaluation: GPT-4.1-mini evaluates each candidate-job pair against five structured criteria:
    1. Location compatibility — candidates within 50km are automatically passed
    2. Salary alignment — maximum €20,000 difference between expectation and offer
    3. Industry sector experience — construction specialisation is weighted heavily
    4. Qualification requirements — 10+ years of experience can substitute formal qualifications
    5. Role and position compatibility — seniority and function match
  • Audit trail: Every evaluation is logged to Google Sheets with a detailed match rationale, so recruiters can see exactly why a candidate was or wasn't shortlisted.
  • Automatic assignment: Candidates who meet the criteria are automatically assigned to the relevant role in RecruitCRM, ready for recruiter review.
  • Slack notification: On completion of a job-triggered run, a Slack message is sent with a summary of matched candidates.
