I'm a Mechatronics graduate who pivoted into AI and automation after realising that most bottlenecks in business are just repetitive human decisions wrapped in a workflow. I've been building systems that remove those bottlenecks ever since.

I've shipped 50+ automation workflows across recruitment, legal, finance, and operations. Most of them are in production today, quietly running in the background while clients focus on the parts of their work that actually need a person.
