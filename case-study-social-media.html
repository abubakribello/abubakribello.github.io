The Challenge

The client was spending 8-10 hours every week manually repurposing long-form webinars and tutorials for LinkedIn, Twitter, and their email newsletter. They needed a way to maintain a high-volume social presence without the manual grind.

The Solution

I built an automated pipeline that takes a single video URL and generates a month's worth of content across all channels.

  • AI Transcription & Analysis: Extracts key insights and "gold nuggets" from raw video.
  • Multi-Platform Writing: Tailors content specifically for LinkedIn threads, Twitter posts, and newsletter digests.
  • Automated Scheduling: Direct integration with content calendars for hands-off publishing.
