AI Voice Agent for Candidate Pre-Qualification

Automating 100+ daily candidate calls to free recruiters from intake work entirely.

Client ToPeople
CRM RecruitCRM
Calls handled 100+/day
Time to live 5 weeks

The situation.

ToPeople, a specialist recruitment firm, was handling over 100 candidate calls per day. Every new applicant needed a pre-qualification call before they could move forward. Recruiters were spending most of their time on this intake work, leaving little room for higher-value tasks like client management and closing placements.

The manual process had three clear problems. Candidates were waiting 2-3 days for a callback. Screening quality varied by recruiter. And the team couldn't scale without hiring more people to do the same repetitive work.

What I built.

I designed and deployed an end-to-end AI voice agent system that automated the entire pre-qualification process without removing the human review layer.

  • Voice agent: Built on ElevenLabs Conversational AI, delivered via Twilio telephony. The agent conducts natural pre-qualification conversations in the same style as a recruiter.
  • RecruitCRM trigger: The workflow fires automatically the moment a new candidate registers in RecruitCRM. No manual initiation required.
  • Structured data extraction: After each call, GPT-4.1-mini processes the transcript and extracts 15+ structured candidate attributes including experience level, availability, salary expectations, location, and sector.
  • CV collection flow: If key information is missing from the call, the system sends a personalised follow-up email requesting the CV. The CV is uploaded to OneDrive, synced to RecruitCRM, and deleted from OneDrive within 5 minutes.
  • Escalation logic: Failed calls don't fall through the cracks. The system automatically retries with a callback, then an SMS, then a final email if the candidate remains unreachable.
  • Error alerting: Any system-level errors trigger an immediate Slack notification so the team can intervene without monitoring the system manually.
