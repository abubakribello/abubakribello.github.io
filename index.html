Client: Construction-focused recruitment agency

The Problem

Recruiters spent 25+ hours per week manually matching candidates to roles. The process was slow, inconsistent, and prevented the team from focusing on client relationships and business development. With growth targets doubling, the manual approach was unsustainable.
