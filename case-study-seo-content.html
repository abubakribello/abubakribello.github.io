The Challenge

A marketing agency was struggling to keep up with client demands for high-quality SEO content. Manually producing each long-form article took 12 hours of research and writing. They were capped at 35 articles per month and couldn't scale without significant hiring.

The Solution

I built an AI-driven content research and generation pipeline that assists writers by handling the heavy lifting of research and initial drafting.

  • Automated SERP Analysis: The system crawls top results for target keywords to identify common themes and content gaps.
  • AI Outline Generation: Creates structured outlines optimized for SEO based on the research data.
  • Intelligent Content Expansion: Generates initial drafts that maintain the agency's voice and style constraints.
