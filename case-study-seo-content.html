Content Repurposing Automation

Turning one piece of content into a full month of scheduled posts across every platform.

Client DigitalXshift
Input formats 3 pipeline paths
Output Scheduled posts across platforms
Time to live 8 weeks

The situation.

DigitalXshift had content across YouTube, LinkedIn, Instagram, and written case studies. Repurposing any of it manually took 8-10 hours per cycle. A long YouTube video might need to become LinkedIn posts, Instagram captions, and email newsletter sections. A case study might become multiple post formats for different platforms.

The team was producing less content than they could because turning raw source material into finished posts was just too slow to do consistently.

What I built.

I built three distinct automation pipelines, each triggered differently, all ending with approved content scheduled and posted automatically.

Path 1 — Long-form YouTube video to multi-platform posts

  • A YouTube URL is submitted and the full transcript is extracted automatically
  • AI segments the transcript into topics and highlights key clips
  • For each segment, the system generates LinkedIn posts, Instagram captions, and email newsletter sections
  • Approved content is queued automatically for scheduling and posting

Path 2 — Case study to on-demand multi-format posts

  • Triggered by an Airtable form submission with case study details
  • AI generates multiple post formats from the same source: written posts, AI infographic briefs, and meme scripts
  • Each format is individually approvable, and approved versions are auto-scheduled

Path 3 — Liked posts repurposed in brand voice

  • A LinkedIn or Instagram post URL is submitted via Airtable form
  • AI adapts the content into an original post written in the DigitalXshift brand voice
  • Approved versions are automatically scheduled and posted
