KissFlow Staff Service Request Portal

Replacing manual email chains with a structured, trackable approval workflow across three departments at OneAcre Fund Nigeria.

Client OneAcre Fund, Nigeria
Platform KissFlow
Departments served 3
Request categories 4 automated

The situation.

OneAcre Fund's Nigeria team was handling staff requests across three departments through email chains and verbal follow-ups. Corporate Operations, IT, and Learning and Development each had their own unstructured process. Requests got sent to the wrong person, sat unread in inboxes, or were never formally logged at all.

Approvals were slow and inconsistent. There was no visibility into where a request stood. Staff had no way to track whether their request had been seen, approved, or rejected. Requests fell through the cracks and had to be chased manually.

What I built.

I built a multi-department KissFlow portal with three distinct request streams, each with structured forms, automated routing, and full approval tracking.

Corporate Operations requests

  • Covers transportation, accommodation, food, and logistics requests for field staff
  • Conditional form fields adjust based on the request type selected
  • Submitted requests route automatically to the Corporate Ops approver
  • Multi-level approval chain based on request type and value

IT service requests

  • Covers hardware, software, and technical support requests
  • Requests route directly to the IT team with request-specific context included
  • Priority and category fields help the tech team triage and respond faster

Learning and Development book borrowing

  • Staff can request books from the L&D library through a structured form
  • Availability and return date tracking built into the workflow
  • Borrowing history is logged for the L&D team

Shared features across all streams

  • Automated email notification to the designated approver on submission
  • One-click approve, reject, or save for reviewers directly from the email notification
  • Status visibility for the submitter at every stage of the approval process
  • Full audit trail of every request, action, and decision
