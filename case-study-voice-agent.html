AI Voice Agent for Pre-Qualification

Automating 200+ monthly candidate calls to save 60 hours of recruiter time

Industry

Timeline

The Challenge

A specialist recruitment firm was handling 200+ monthly candidate pre-qualification calls, consuming over 60 hours of recruiter time. The manual process created several critical bottlenecks:

  • Recruiters spent 60+ hours monthly on repetitive pre-qualification calls
  • Response time to candidates averaged 2-3 days, causing candidate drop-off
  • Inconsistent screening quality across different recruiters
  • High-value recruiters were stuck doing low-value administrative work
  • Scaling the team would require significant hiring costs

The firm needed a solution that could handle the volume while maintaining quality and improving response times.

The Solution

I designed and deployed an AI-powered voice agent system that automated the entire pre-qualification process:

System Architecture

  • Intelligent Voice Agent: Custom-built AI voice agent using advanced natural language processing to conduct natural, conversational pre-qualification calls
  • Automated Scheduling: Integration with the firm's ATS to automatically trigger calls when new candidates applied
  • Structured Data Capture: Voice agent extracted and recorded key qualification criteria including experience, availability, salary expectations, and location preferences
  • Smart Routing: Qualified candidates were automatically routed to appropriate recruiters based on specialization and availability
  • CRM Integration: All call data, recordings, and transcripts automatically synced to the firm's CRM system
  • Quality Assurance: Built-in monitoring and human-in-the-loop checkpoints for edge cases

Key Features

  • Natural conversation flow with context awareness
  • Multi-language support for diverse candidate pools
  • Automatic call recording and transcription
  • Real-time qualification scoring
  • Fallback to human recruiter for complex scenarios
  • 24/7 availability for candidate convenience
