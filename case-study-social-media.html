SmartHub 360 — Legal Document Automation

Cutting 25-hour testament cases down to 5 minutes and tripling client capacity for a Dubai law firm.

Client SmartHub 360, Dubai
Industry Legal
Capacity increase 3x
Time to live 5 weeks

The situation.

SmartHub 360, a Dubai-based law firm specialising in testaments and estate planning, was spending 12-25 hours per case on document collection, manual data entry, and client follow-up. With 20 active clients per month, the workload was unsustainable.

Every new client triggered a long back-and-forth: collecting documents by email, manually entering data, drafting the testament, chasing the client for approvals. Leads were dropping off because response times were slow. Conversion sat at around 15% because the intake experience felt slow and unorganised.

What I built.

I built an end-to-end automation system covering intake, document generation, lawyer review, and lead management, all connected and running without manual coordination between steps.

  • Smart intake form (Jotform): A conditional Jotform that adapts to each case type and collects all required client data and documents upfront. No more email back-and-forth to gather basic information.
  • Automated document generation: The moment a form is submitted, a testament draft is generated automatically from the submission data. The first draft is ready in minutes, not days.
  • Lawyer review workflow: The generated draft is routed to the assigned lawyer with an automated notification. The lawyer reviews and approves rather than creating from scratch.
  • Lead management in HighLevel: Every lead enters a structured pipeline in HighLevel. Automated follow-up sequences, invoice issuance, payment confirmation, and Jotform link delivery are all triggered at the right stage without anyone manually initiating them.
  • Automated stage updates: Every client status change triggers the appropriate next action in HighLevel automatically. No manual pipeline management required.
  • ZIWO phone integration: The ZIWO phone system is integrated with HighLevel so all call records appear inside HighLevel, linked to the relevant client or lead. Call history is visible in context without switching tools.
