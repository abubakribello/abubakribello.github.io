The Challenge

The founder of a growing service business was spending 6-8 hours every week on manual bookkeeping across multiple payment channels (Stripe, PayPal, Bank Transfers). This was taking time away from strategic growth and high-level client management.

The Solution

I built a unified financial dashboard and automation engine that consolidates data from all sources into a single source of truth.

  • Unified Data Ingestion: Automated triggers capture every transaction from Stripe and PayPal in real-time.
  • Categorization Engine: AI-powered rules categorize expenses and income automatically based on historical data.
  • Real-time Dashboard: A visual dashboard providing instant visibility into cash position, monthly recurring revenue (MRR), and burn rate.
