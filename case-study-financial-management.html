The situation.

DigitalXshift, a fast-growing consultancy, was reconciling transactions manually across three different revenue streams. Invoices and receipts arrived via Gmail. Sales data sat in HighLevel. There was no single view of their cash position at any point in time.

Month-end reconciliation took hours. The founders had no way to quickly answer basic questions like "how much came in this week" or "what's the current burn rate." Financial decisions were being made on outdated information.

What I built.

I built an automated ingestion and dashboarding system that pulls all financial data into one live view, without anyone touching a spreadsheet.

  • Gmail ingestion: The system polls Gmail automatically and extracts key data from incoming invoices and receipts using AI. No manual logging or forwarding required.
  • HighLevel sales sync: Deal and payment data is pulled from HighLevel automatically, covering all closed sales and payment milestones.
  • Categorisation engine: Each transaction is classified by type and project using AI, so the dashboard always reflects accurate category breakdowns without manual tagging.
  • Live Google Sheets dashboard: A single view shows running totals, revenue vs expenses by category, and burn rate updated in real time. No manual entry required to keep it current.
  • Anomaly alerts: Unusual transactions trigger an alert so the team can review them without having to audit the sheet themselves.
